v1 → v2 : Three Steps of Improvement

Step 1

Introduce Interfaces

Define AccountRepository and TransferService interfaces.

Service depends on abstraction, not concrete class. Enables multiple implementations.

Design fix OCP
Step 2

Use Factory

AccountRepositoryFactory creates the right implementation by type.

Service no longer does new JdbcAccountRepository() inside its methods.

Decouples creation
Step 3

Constructor DI

Dependency is injected via constructor by the assembler (main).

Service doesn't create or lookup — it just receives.

Performance fix DIP
